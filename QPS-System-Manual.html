1. Introduction

1.1 About Quick Property Services

Quick Property Services (QPS) is a web-based platform that connects homeowners with qualified vendors for home repair and remodeling projects. The system streamlines the entire process from service request submission to project completion and payment.

How It Works Homeowners submit service requests → Admins assign qualified vendors → Vendors submit competitive estimates → Admin selects the best estimate and applies markup → Homeowner accepts → Work is completed → Payment is released.

1.2 User Roles

Role Description Access
Homeowner Property owners who submit service requests and track project progress Public website & Homeowner Portal
Vendor Service providers who receive assignments, submit estimates, and complete work Vendor Portal
Admin System administrators who manage requests, assign vendors, and oversee operations Admin Dashboard

1.3 System Requirements

  • A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari)
  • Stable internet connection
  • Valid email address for account registration

3. Homeowner Guide

3.1 Submitting a Service Request

To request a home service, follow these steps:

1
Go to "Submit Request"
Click the "Submit Request" link from the homepage or navigation menu.
2
Fill in Your Information
Complete the form with the required details.
3
Upload Photos/Videos (Optional)
Attach images or videos showing the issue. Max 10MB per image, 50MB per video.
4
Submit the Request
Click "Submit Request" to send your request. You will receive a unique tracking code (e.g., QPS-A1B2C3D4).

Request Form Fields

Full Name Your complete name Required
Email Address Valid email for account and notifications Required
Phone Number Contact phone number Required
Password Minimum 6 characters (creates your account) Required
Property Address Full address of the property Required
Service Category Plumbing, Electrical, Painting, Carpentry, Roofing, HVAC, Landscaping, Cleaning, Flooring, or General Maintenance
Description Detailed description of the work needed Required
Photos/Videos Upload images or videos of the issue (optional)
Important Save your tracking code! You will need it to track your request status and view estimates.

3.2 Tracking Your Request

You can track your request at any time using your tracking code:

1
Go to the "Track Request" page from the navigation menu.
2
Enter your tracking code (e.g., QPS-A1B2C3D4).
3
View your request status, estimate details, and completion information.
Tip You can also log in to your Homeowner Dashboard to see all your requests in one place without needing tracking codes.

3.3 Reviewing & Accepting Estimates

Once the admin sends you an estimate, you can review and respond:

1
Track your request or log into your dashboard.
2
Review the estimate details including price, work description, and timeline.
3
Click "Accept Estimate" to approve or "Decline" to reject.

3.4 Homeowner Dashboard

The Homeowner Dashboard provides an overview of all your service requests with their current statuses. From here you can:

  • View all submitted requests
  • Check the current status of each request
  • View estimates and accept/decline them
  • See completion reports with photos and videos

4. Vendor Guide

4.1 Vendor Dashboard

After logging in, the Vendor Dashboard displays all your assigned service requests. Each request card shows:

  • Homeowner name and property address
  • Service description and category
  • Attached photos/videos from the homeowner
  • Current request status

4.2 Viewing Assigned Requests

When an admin assigns you to a request, it will appear on your dashboard. Click on a request to see the full details including:

  • Complete description of the work needed
  • Property address and homeowner contact info
  • Photos and videos of the issue

4.3 Submitting Estimates

For each assigned request, you can submit a competitive estimate:

1
Open the Request
Click on an assigned request from your dashboard.
2
Fill in the Estimate Form
Provide a detailed work description, your estimated price, and the expected timeline.
3
Attach Supporting Media (Optional)
Upload photos or videos to support your estimate.
4
Submit
Click "Submit Estimate" to send your estimate to the admin for review.

Estimate Form Fields

Work Description Detailed description of the proposed work Required
Estimated Price Your cost for completing the work Required
Timeline Expected duration (e.g., "5-7 business days") Required
Photos/Videos Supporting media for your estimate (optional)

4.4 Submitting Completion Reports

Once the work is finished, submit a completion report as proof:

1
Navigate to the completed request from your dashboard.
2
Click "Submit Completion Report".
3
Provide a detailed description of the work completed.
4
Upload photos and/or videos showing the completed work (before & after).
5
Click "Submit". The request status will automatically update to "Completed".
Tip Always include clear before-and-after photos to help verify work completion and expedite payment release.

4

Note You can remove a vendor assignment only if they have not yet submitted an estimate.

5.4 Handling Estimates & Markup

After vendors submit their estimates:

1
Review all estimates submitted by assigned vendors (price, description, timeline).
2
Select the best estimate by clicking "Select" next to it.
3
Apply markup percentage to determine the homeowner's final price.
4
Click "Send Estimate to Homeowner" to share the final price.

Markup Calculation

Formula Final Price = Vendor Price + (Vendor Price × Markup %)

Example: If the vendor estimates $1,000 and you apply a 20% markup:
Final Price = $1,000 + ($1,000 × 0.20) = $1,200

6. Request Lifecycle & Statuses

Every service request progresses through the following statuses:

# Status Description Action By
1 New Request submitted, awaiting admin review Homeowner
2 Reviewing Admin is reviewing the request details Admin
3 Vendors Assigned Admin has assigned vendors to the request Admin
4 Estimates Received Vendors have submitted their estimates Vendor
5 Estimate Sent Admin selected an estimate and sent it to the homeowner Admin
6 Homeowner Accepted Homeowner accepted the estimate Homeowner
7 Payment Received Payment received and held in escrow Admin
8 In Progress Vendor is actively working on the project Admin
9 Completed Vendor submitted completion report Vendor
10 Vendor Paid Payment released to vendor — request is finalized Admin

Visual Flow

New → Reviewing → Vendors Assigned → Estimates Received → Estimate Sent → Homeowner Accepted → Payment Received → In Progress → Completed → Vendor Paid

7. Payment Workflow

The system uses an escrow-style payment model to protect both homeowners and vendors:

Payment Status Description What Happens
Pending Awaiting homeowner payment Homeowner has accepted estimate but not yet paid
Paid (Escrow) Payment received and held Payment is held securely until work is completed and verified
Released Payment sent to vendor Admin releases payment after verifying completion report
How Escrow Protects Everyone
  • Homeowners: Payment is only released after work is verified as complete
  • Vendors: Payment is guaranteed once the work is done and approved

Payment Flow

Estimate Accepted → Homeowner Pays → Held in Escrow → Work Completed → Payment Released

8. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I get my tracking code?

Your tracking code is displayed immediately after you submit a service request. It follows the format QPS-XXXXXXXX. Make sure to save it for future reference.

Q: Can I track my request without logging in?

Yes. Go to the "Track Request" page and enter your tracking code. No login is required to view your request status.

Q: How many vendors can be assigned to my request?

Up to 5 vendors can be assigned to a single request. This ensures competitive estimates and gives the admin options to choose the best one for you.

Q: As a vendor, how do I receive new assignments?

New assignments appear automatically on your Vendor Dashboard after an admin assigns you. Log in regularly to check for new requests.

Q: How is the final price determined?

The admin selects the best vendor estimate and applies a service markup percentage. The final price shown to the homeowner includes both the vendor's cost and the platform fee.

Q: What happens if I decline an estimate?

If you decline, the admin will be notified and may send a revised estimate or assign different vendors to provide new estimates.

Q: How do I know when my project is complete?

When the vendor submits a completion report with photos/videos, the status updates to "Completed." You can view the completion report on your tracking page or dashboard.

Q: How do I add a new vendor to the system?

Admins can add vendors from the Admin Dashboard by navigating to Vendors → Add Vendor and filling in the vendor's details.

Q: Is my payment secure?

Yes. The escrow system holds your payment until the work is verified as complete. Funds are only released to the vendor after the admin confirms completion.
